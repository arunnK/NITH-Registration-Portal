On this NIMBUS Team .eXe presents a basic working prototype of registration portal for the students of NITH, which will help us a lot in the Semester Registration Process by facilitating the Fee Payment Procedure and clearing library dues. For payment register on site, login into your account and go to the option of submitting Tuition fee, do the payment using Banking card, after the successful transaction go for the payment of hostel fees followed by the library dues if any. After successful payments view and submit the form .After successful submission you will be registered for the Semester once your filled details are verified by the Department staffs and Library staff. This Registration portal will provide convenience to student and will save the time.
